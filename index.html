Squadra Corse is the name of Automobili Lamborghini’

motorsport department. It was created to offer unparalleled support to Automobili Lamborghini’s customers in the field. The cars that compete in the most prestigious GT championships all over the world are designed and produced in-house by Squadra Corse. It also organizes the Lamborghini Super Trofeo: the fastest one-make championship in the world.
